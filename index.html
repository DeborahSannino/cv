Localization ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Be conscious of the global elements in your dreams. When starting local, dream of taking it global sooner.” ― Israelmore Ayivor, Shaping the dream

Localization is often confused with translation, but these terms actually mean two different things. Translation refers to the conversion of written text from one language to another, while localization is the process of making a product or message resonate with a specific target culture—as if it were created there in the first place. In this process time zones, money, national holidays, local color sensitivities, product or service name translation, gender roles and geographic references must all be considered.
